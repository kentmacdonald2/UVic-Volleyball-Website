UVIC Volleyball Club

At the University of Victoria

The UVic Volleyball Club is an opportunity for players of all levels to participate in a drop-in style evening of volleyball. The club is designed to facilitate co-ed six on six games. All players join as an individual/single and get organized into different teams based on weekly turnout. Up to three courts are set up and are stratified according to skill, experience, and competition level. New players are welcome to join and learn to play through the game time and informal coaching sessions.

All equipment is provided for students.

To join the club, a $10.00 membership fee per semester, or $20.00 membership fee for both semesters can be paid directly through the front desk at either McKinnon gym, or the CARSA membership services desk.

The club is always looking for more members! If you are passionate about volleyball, want to improve your skills or are interested in the sport, contact us!

Information

What?

The UVic Volleyball Club is the center for all things volleyball at UVic. We offer drop in volleyball for a wide variety of skill levels, from beginner to advanced!

Where?

McKinnon Gym at the University of Victoria

When?

Most Sundays at 6:30. Check our facebook page for last-minute club cancellations.

How? (Can I Join)

1. Give our facebook page a "like"
2. Come out to club on Sunday.
3. Register at Mckinnon or CARSA
